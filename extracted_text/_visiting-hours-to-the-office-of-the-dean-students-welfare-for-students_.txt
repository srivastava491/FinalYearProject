Visiting Hours for Students to the office of the Dean (Students’ Welfare) – National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra Visiting Hours for Students to the office of the Dean (Students’ Welfare) IMPORTANT NOTICE It has been observed that daily many students of the Institute visit to the office of the  Dean (Students’ Welfare) for their official and personal issues related work during all the times which affects the productivity of the office and due to this  the time bound work of the office of the Dean (Students’ Welfare) is delayed most of the time. In view of the above, the students are hereby advised to follow the below mentioned schedule for visiting the office of the Dean (Students’ Welfare) for any official/personal issue  strictly. The students facing critical issues /time bound matters may visit the office of the Dean (Students’ Welfare) any time during office hours. Sr. No. Days Time 1. Monday to Friday (Except Public Holidays) From 04:30 pm to 05:30 pm 2. Saturday (Except Public Holidays) From 11:00 am to 12: 30 pm The students are advised to follow the above time strictly except in the case of emergency.
