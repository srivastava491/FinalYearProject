CCMT-CCMN – National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra CCMT-CCMN CCMT is a common platform for candidates to apply for M.Tech./ M.Arch./ M.Plan. programmes, based on their GATE score of years 2021, 2022 and 2023, in all NITs, IIEST Shibpur, IIITs and some GFTIs. For details, please refer to the  CCMT website. On the other hand, CCMN is the platform for candidates to apply for M.Sc./M.Sc.(Tech.) programmes, based on their JAM score of year 2023, in NITs, IIEST Shibpur, IIITs and some GFTIs. For details, please refer to the CCMN website. These centralized systems maintained by NIC, Govt. of India, provide a common and convenient platform for online counselling wherein the candidates can fill-in online application form from their home and apply to all programmes in all the participating institutions (PIs) to which they are eligible. For the academic session 2023-24, Ministry of Education, Govt. of India has assigned National Institute of Technology Kurukshetra the task of coordinating the admissions through CCMT-2023 and CCMN-2023.
