NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY KURUKSHETRA OFFICE OF THE DEAN (STUDENTS' WELFARE) No. Dean (SW)24/439 With warm greetings from the Institution! GOLDEN JUBILEE CELEBRATION OF B,Sc. (ENGG) 1974-79 BATCH Golden Jubilee Celebrations of batch 1974-79 was celebrated in the Institute on completion of 50 years from admission year ie from 1974 to 2024 during 29.11.2024 to 01.12.2024.The Institute appreciates emotions and contribution of the batch towards the welfare of the students in particular and Institute in general. The Institute is regularly inviting the distinguished Alumni to share their rich experience with the students of the Institute from time to time so that their professional development can be enhanced resulting in better placement prospects. In this regard, the Institute is planning to reserve 2 hour slot on every Wednesday in the time table from even semester of the Academic Year 2024-25 onwards for "Wednesday Colloquium" where the distinguished Alumni wil be invited for interaction to the students in hybrid mode Copy to: Dated: 03.12.2024 It is further clarified that the Institute has not authorized any person or Alumni Association of this Institute to accept donations, gifts, endowment funds etc from the Alumni of RECK / NITKKR. Institute is responsible for any donations, gifts, endowment funds etc from the Alumni of RECK / NITKKR received in the official bank account of the institute. The details of the bank account is available at the Alumni-> tab on the Institute website. We look forward to your active participation in the overall development of the institution and help us in shaping it into one of the best global institutions. Kindly also keep on looking at various announcements on the Institute website (www.nitkkr.ac.in) under Alumni tab. 1. Assistant Registrar to the Director for the kind information of the Director. 2.
