NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY (Under the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India) KURUKSHETRA-136119 Advt.No.24/2024 Advertisement for the Internship (2 months to 6 months) Applications in the prescribed format are invited from external students for an Internship of 2 months to 6 months to work in the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Govt. of India sponsored project titled “Information Security Education and Awareness (ISEA) Project Phase-Ill" at Department of Computer Engineering. The internship will be governed by the funding agency (MeitY) rules & NIT Kurukshetra. The details of the advertisement are given below: Title of the Project : Information Security Education and Awareness (ISEA) Project Phase-Ill Principal Investigator : Dr. Mayank Dave, Professor, Department of Computer Engineering, NIT Kurukshetra Number of Internships : Up to 05 (Five) Duration : 2 months to 6 months Stipend : Rs. 20,000/- (Consolidated) Essential Qualifications : Pursuing B.Tech./B.E. (5th/7th Semester) or Integrated B.Tech./B.E. (5th/7th/9th Semester) or M.Tech./M.E. (2nd Year) in Computer Science and Engineering/ Computer Engineering /Information Technology/Cyber Security/ Information Security or equivalent with 7.5 grades point (out of 10) / 70% marks. Desirable Qualifications : 1) Proficiency in Python / Java / Linux 2) Experience in simulation tools related to Computer, Network and Information Security. 3) Good analytical ability and competent in report writing Mode of Internship : Physical at NIT Kurukshetra Accommodation and Food : The institute will provide the facility for food and accommodation facility depending upon availability which will be borne by the selected candidate as per the institute norms. Benefits to Students : The selected students will have good opportunity to work in a national project in the area of Computer Network and Security. If the student is willing to complete the B.Tech. Final Year project / M.Tech. Dissertation work, an extension in internship may be allowed without stipend.
