NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY (Under the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India) KURUKSHETRA-136119 Recruitment of Project Staff on Purely Contract Basis Advt. No.32/2023 Applications, on purely contract basis, are invited on prescribed format for the post of Field Investigator for sponsored project of Indian Council of Social Science Research entitled "Impact Assessment of PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Scheme". The application form and the details of all educational qualifications and relevant experience required for various positions are available on Institute website http://www.nitkkr.ac.in. The scanned copy of duly filled and signed application form along with self-attested mark-sheets of all semesters, relevant experience certificates and necessary documents must be submitted in a single PDF file by email at ashwani@nitkkr.ac.in on or before 27th December, 2023. The e-mail must contain the subject as "Application for the post of Field Staff or Research Assistant or Research Associate". Applications received after said date will be not considered. Eligibility Criterion: Post Qualification No. of Posts Duration Consolidated Pay (Per Month) Field Investigator Post graduate in social science discipline with minimum 55% 01 3 Months Rs.30,000/-p.m. Note: 1. The short-listed candidates will be called for walk-in Interview on 28.12.2023. Interview will be organized in hybrid mode. 2. The qualification and experience may be relaxed at any point of time by the Institute for otherwise exceptional candidates. 3. No TA/DA will be paid for appearing in the Interview. 4. For the post of Filed Investigator, candidates having knowledge of Tamil will be preferred. Sd/- Registrar I/c
