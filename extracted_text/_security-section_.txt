Security Section – National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra Security Section Security & Surveillance Section “The Security & Surveillance Section” at NIT Kurukshetra, under the able vision of Professor I/C Prof VK Bajpai,  is dedicated and determined to warrant a comprehensive security coverage for both people and property across the Institute, ensuring strict adherence to security protocols. Under the direction of Ajeet Singh Yadav, Security Officer of the institute, the section is resourced with 04 Security Supervisors, 11 Armed Security Guards, 127 Security Guards, and 01 CCTV Control Room Operator. The team comprises of highly trained professionals with a strong background in defence forces. In order to get best out of them, we ensure on-campus training to these personnel on various fronts like their daily operations, cultural diversity, sexual harassment, anti-ragging protocols, emergency procedures, first aid, fire prevention and fighting, event security, community policing, and crime prevention. To support their duties, security personnel are equipped with dedicated vehicles, including Gypsies and bicycles, for patrolling both on and off-campus. The security staff is responsible for monitoring the campus perimeter, gates, static posts, and conducting regular patrols of the campus. A 24/7 Mobile Security Control Room, equipped with advanced technologies, ensures rapid response to service calls and enhances the efficiency of security operations. Key technologies in use include: 240 CCTV cameras installed across the campus to ensure the safety of students, staff, and institute property. Round-the-clock security presence at all campus entry points, where guards inspect vehicles before entry. Security Helpline: 90346-51015 Wireless Communication systems for constant coordination. The Security Section maintains direct communication with local emergency services such as the local Police, Fire Brigade, and nearby Hospitals. Adopting a community-based approach, the Security Section works to prevent crime, assist victims, and investigate incidents in collaboration with campus authorities and law enforcement agencies. It partners with departments such as Works and the Health Centre to ensure a safe environment for students, faculty, and staff, enabling a positive academic and social experience. The section also works closely with student representatives, faculty, and staff via the NIT Employees Union, Officers Association, and Faculty Forum. Each year, the Security Section conducts security and safety awareness training for incoming students during the Orientation session. The entire team at the Security Section welcomes you to NIT Kurukshetra and is committed to ensuring your safety during your time here. Please do not hesitate to reach out if you have any concerns. Remember, maintaining security is a shared responsibility between the NIT Security Section and the community.
