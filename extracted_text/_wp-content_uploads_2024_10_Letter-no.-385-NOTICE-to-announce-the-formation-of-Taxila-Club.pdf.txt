No. Dean (SW))/24/85 Institute is excited to announce the formation of Taxila Club, a new student club at NIT Kurukshetra, powered by the Yuj Foundation, which was founded by NIT Kurukshetra alumni. This club is designed to provide students with opportunities for leadership development, personal growth, and hands-on project experiences. The Yuj Foundation, with its extensive experience in life education, entrepreneurship, and skill-building programs, will mentor and support the Taxila Clutb in various initiatives. As part of this collaboration, the club will offer Hands-on experience in over 350 courses and numerouS projects for personal and professional growth. Students from all years and streams are cordially invited to attend the orientation and registration session. Faculty members are also invited to join us in this special event.
