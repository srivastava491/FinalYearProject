Degree programmes are required to choose one Open Elective (OE) OR Programme 2 nd Elective (PE) course to be studied in the semester (Even Semester) of Academic Session 2024-25 as per syllabi and scheme of the M.Tech. Degree programmes. The Open Elective is mandatory for all specializations of Mechanical Engg. Deptt. (as per scheme and syllabi) only. For other specialization students, it is optional and these students can optfor Open Elective or can study Programme Elective of their department. The Open Elective course code, name of the course and no. of seats available for the students of various disciplines/Departments are mentioned in the enclosed list. The students who are interested to study Open Elective instead of Programme Elective are advised to fill/indicate their maximum preferences/choices as applicable to their M.Tech. programme in the Google form (link for Open Elective for 2 nd semester) latest by 24.12.2024 (Tuesday), 05.aO.PM positively. The students are required to fill the form using nitkkr domain email id only. It is to be noted that one student can fill one form only, therefore please fill the form with utmost care. The file name of the uploaded documents (in pdf format) must be the roll number of the student and the size of the document is maximum 01 MB (i.e. 324102101.pdD It is possible that some courses may not be offered if the number of students opting for the courses is less than 5. In such cases the students would be allotted next preference opted by them if seats are available. In case student is not in a position for allocation as per his/her choice and merit based on GATE score/Qualifying degree marks or CGPA, he/she has to study program elective offered by his/her department/School It is requested to carefully fill ma'<imum choices applicable to his/her branch in his/her preferred order. The Academic Section will not able to change courses one allocation is made.
