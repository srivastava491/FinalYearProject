Institute’s Innovation Council (IIC) aligned with MoE’s Innovation Cell (MIC) – National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra Institute’s Innovation Council (IIC) aligned with MoE’s Innovation Cell (MIC) NIT Kurukshetra convenes the members of the Institute’s Innovation Council (IIC), which is aligned with the Ministry of Education’s Innovation Cell (MIC). The IIC will be an umbrella entity offering a range of development programs, workshops, etc Notification of Institute’s Innovation Council (IIC)
