DEPARTMENT OF MATHEMATICS NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY KURUKSHETRA- 136119 No.: MA/2024/52.5 Copy to: Engagement of Guest Faculty The Department of Mathematics invites applications for Guest Faculty positions (purely on a temporary basis) to meet out the teaching load requirement for UG (B.Tech.) classes during the Even Semester of Academic Year 2024-25 on a per lecture basis @1500/- per lecture plus 10% extra for examination related evaluation work subject to a mnaximum amount of Rs 70,900/- per month for candidate with Ph.D. degree and Rs 57,700/- for the candidates who have submitted their Ph.D. thesis. Two tutorials/ seminars will be treated as equivalent to one lecture for the purpose of honorarium/remuneration. Walk-in-Interview The qualification for Guest Faculty shall be "First class both at UG and PG level (60% or 6.5/10 CGPA) with Ph.D. in Mathematics". The selected candidates will have to complete all related evaluation & examination works along with the engagement of classes. The interested candidates must submit their application through Google form in the prescribed application form along with self-attested copies of certificates and marks sheets either by post or in person in the office of the Department or through e-mail at hodmaths@nitkkr.ac,in on or before December 17, 2024 (Tuesday) by 05:00 PM. The application forms received after the last date and time will not be considered. The presentation, followed by an interview for the same, shall be held on December 18, 2024, at 10:00 AM in the office of the Head of the Department. All are requested to bring all original testimonials at the time of the interview. All communications with the eligible candidates shall be made by e-mail.
