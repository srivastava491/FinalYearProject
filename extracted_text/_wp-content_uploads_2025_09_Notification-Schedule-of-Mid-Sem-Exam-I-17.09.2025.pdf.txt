NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY KURUKSHETRA No. (Acad.)/Exam./2025-26/1313 Dated: 17.09.2025 NOTIFICATION The Mid-Semester Exam-I for all the UG, PG, and Ph.D. programmes will be conducted from 24.09.2025 (Wednesday) to 26.09.2025 (Friday) for the Odd Semester of the Academic Year 2025-26. (A) Mid Sem Exam Duration & Slots The respective course coordinator may schedule the exam within the time slot allotted and complete all the processes of the exam within the time slot. The course coordinator may schedule the Mid-Semester Exam on any other date in case of unforeseen situations, with intimation to the examinee concerned by email or mobile communication. (B) Invigilation/Supervision duties for Offline Exam Faculty members teaching the course may be assigned duties for the Mid-Semester Exam conduct/ invigilation/ supervision, etc., for these examinations by the concerned course coordinators in consultation with the respective HoDs/Coordinators. However, if any department/school does not have sufficient faculty, the Head of the concerned department may take the help of faculty (Regular and others) from other departments. (C) Sessional Improvement of courses under old & new schemes of UG & PG programmes The respective course coordinator of the courses of the old scheme will also conduct the Mid-Semester Exam (Sessional Improvement) for the students having re-appear in the sessional of the even and odd semester courses UG & PG programmes, which are not being offered in the respective semesters, as per the scheme and syllabus applicable for them. As decided by the Senate in its 50th Meeting held on 25-07-2023 vide Agenda Item No. –S 50.05 and subsequent approval of the Chairman Senate on dated 31-08-2023, the conduct of examinations of courses of even and odd semesters together in every semester is discontinued for B.Tech. programmes for the B.Tech.-2023 batch onwards. The odd semester re-appear examinations for students studying B.Tech 1st semester onwards from AY 2023-24 will be held in odd semesters, and even semester reappear examinations will be held in even semesters. The students admitted before AY 2023-24 and not repeating the 1st semester onwards from AY 2023-24 will have an option of appearing in re-appear examinations of odd semester courses in even semester and vice versa. The cut list for re-appear examinees (old and new scheme) will be sent to the respective HoDs/School Coordinators by Email on or before 19-09-2025. Sd/- Faculty In-Charge (Academic) Copy to: 1.
