NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY (Under the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India) KURUKSHETRA-136119 Advt.No.09/2025 Recruitment of Project Staff on a Purely Contract Basis Applications are invited from Indian nationals for temporary Research Assistant and Field Investigator positions under the ICSSR-sponsored research project. The details of the project and the posts for recruitment are as follows: Title of the Project : Impact Assessment of Pradhan Mantri Street Vendor's AtmaNirbhar Nidhi Yojna (PM SVANidhi) on Street Vendors in Urban Haryana Sponsored by : Indian council of Social Science research (ICSSR), Gov. of India. Principal Investigator : Dr. Geeta Sachdeva, Dept. of Humanities & Social Sciences, NIT Kurukshetra. Name of posts & Educational Qualifications of Project Staff: Post Qualification No. of posts Duration Consolidated pay (per month) Research Assistant Post Graduate in Social Science Discipline with a minimum 55% with NET/M.Phil/Ph.D 01 10-11 Months Rs. 37,000/- p.m. Field Investigator Post Graduate in Social Science Discipline with a minimum 55% 01 03-04 months Rs. 20,000/- p.m. How to apply: The application form and the details of all educational qualifications and relevant experience for various positions are available on the Institute Website http://www.nitkkr.ac.in. The scanned copy of the duly filled and signed application form along with self-attested mark sheets of all semesters, relevant experience certificates, and other necessary documents, must be submitted as a single PDF file via email to geetasachdeva@nitkkr.ac.in on or before 01.05.2025. The email must contain the subject as “Application for the post of Research Assistant or Field Investigator”. Applications received after said date will not be considered. Note: 1. The short-listed candidates will be called for a walk-in Interview through e-mail correspondence only. 2. Candidates shall bring along with them the original degrees/certificates (s) and experience certificate(s) at the time of interview for verification. 3. No TA/DA will be paid for appearing in the Interview 4. The list of selected candidates will be displayed on the institute website within one week after the interview is held. Sd/- Registrar I/c
