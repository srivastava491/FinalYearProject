NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY KURUKSHETRA No. Acad./2024/2058 Dated: 10.12.2024 NOTIFICATION In continuation of the notification no. Acad./2024/1458, dated: 03.09.2024, the minor degree courses are going to start from the AY 2024-25 onwards. The course "Unmanned Aerial Systems and Applications" in the minor degree programme is being offered by the Department of Electrical Engineering to the students of B.Tech. 3rd year (All branches) without any fee. The students can show their interest by filling the Google form through the link as mentioned below: Google Form Link: https://forms.gle/sjMEjQ9GxZ4eS4VG7 The students are required to read the instructions carefully before filling in the choices in Google Forms (Refer to Annexure A). This is for information and further necessary action of all concerned. Sd/- Faculty I/C (Academic) Copy to: 1. All HoDs 2. Dean (Acad.) 3. Chief Warden (Boys & Girls) 4. Prof. I/C (CCN) with a request to get it to be uploaded on Institute website. 5. AR to Director for kind information of the Director Annexure - A IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS: 1. Last date of Application is 20.12.2024 (till 5 PM). 2. The seats will be allotted to the students on the basis of choices filled by the students. 3. The allotted minor degree course in a particular specialization will remain same from 5th to 8th semester of B.Tech. 4. The applicants have to choose courses from the available course list. 5. The student is allowed to choose only one minor degree course offered by the Other Departments (except parent department). However, minor degree course in Electrical Engineering (Unmanned Aerial Systems and Applications) is open for all the departments. 6. No fee is required for the course "Unmanned Aerial Systems and Applications" in the minor degree programme offered by the Department of Electrical Engineering. 7. The students have to complete/pass the minor degree courses of 12 to 16 credits for the award of a minor degree in a particular specialization. 8. The classes of both 5th and 6th semesters (i.e. odd and even) semester of the AY 2024-25 will run in the 6th semester (even semester) only. 9. The classes of 7th and 8th semesters of AY 2025-26 will be in the respective semesters (odd and even both). 10. The classes of minor degree courses will be conducted on weekends (i.e. Saturday & Sunday).
