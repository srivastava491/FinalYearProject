No. Dean (SW)/2024/4S1 NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY KURUKSHETRA The Senate in its 36th meeting held on 21.9.2019 vide item no. S 36.14 approved the proposal for institution of "RECK-66 Alumni Scholarships" for economically weaker B. Tech. students from "RECK-66 Alumni Trust" from academic year 2019-20. The scholarship of Rs. 20,000/- each will be awarded every year to four first year B.
