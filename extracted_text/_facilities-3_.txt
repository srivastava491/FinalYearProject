Facilities – National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra Facilities n Energy Lab, the purchase order of three equipments is placed and the test rigs will be installed very soon. The equipments are: Solar Thermal Training System: Objective is to determine the collector efficiency factor & heat removal factor of flat plate collector in thermo symphonic mode and forced mode of flow with different input parameters and radiation level.Solar Concentrator Training System: Objective is to determine the collector efficiency factor & heat removal factor of parabolic Trough collector with different input parameters and radiation level.Wind Turbine with Blower set up: Objective is to simulate the wind turbine characteristics in laboratory. The purchase of various other lab equipments and software is in process and will be completed in year 2017.
