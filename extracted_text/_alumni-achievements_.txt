Alumni Achievements – National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra Alumni Achievements Our Alumnus 1982…87 batch Sandeep Gupta was CEO of this project. Proud of him. Heartiest congratulations to him on behalf of Alumni Cell of institute It is a matter of pleasure that our esteemed Alumnus Mr. Arvind Goel…Chairman Tata auto components….1974…79 batch represented India along with India Loabour..Youth and Sports Minister at ILO. Indian state ministers and select ambassadors from embassies based out of Geneva plus Ministers from African countries. It is a matter of great pride and honour for the Institute that one of its 1986  Mechanical passout Alumni, Er. Rajesh Pandey is promoted by the President of India to the post of Principal Chief Commissioner/Principal Director General of Customs & Indirect Taxes, Government of India, which is the highest post in the Indian Revenue Service (IRS) cadre of the country. The institute feels proud of the achievements of its alumni. The Institute congratulates Er. Rajesh Pandey for his marvellous achievements in his career and expresses its best wishes to him.
