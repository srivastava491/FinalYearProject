Nitish Aggarwal, IPS Superintendent of Police 01744-233903 spkrk@hry.nic.in IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS TO THE FIRST YEAR STUDENTS There will be an Orientation program conducted by the office of the Dean (SW) for the newly admitted students. The schedule of the Orientation program will be notified on the Institute website (nitkkr.ac.in) under Students’ Welfare->Notifications/Notices. Please visit the Institute website regularly. The first year students are bonafide students of the Institute. They have the right to use the privileges available for the bonafide students. The first year students should desist from doing anything, with or against their will, even if ordered by the senior students, and that any attempt of ragging shall be prominently reported to the Anti-Ragging Squad or to the Hostel Warden or to the Head of the Institute. There will be a ‘get together’ event of the freshers and seniors organized and monitored by the office of the Dean (Students’ Welfare) for making strong bonds among the freshers and seniors. This ‘get together’ event will also serve the purpose of joint counselling of freshers and seniors.
