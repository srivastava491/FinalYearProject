SIEMENS CENTRE OF EXCELLENCE NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY KURUKSHETRA Training Program Overview Certification Short term course on Industrial Robot and Plcs. All registered participants will be awarded certification from SCoE, NIT Kurukshetra. Eligibility Diploma, B.Tech. M.Tech (All branches) Learning Objectives: The trainee will learn to operate and understand the fundamental working principles of industrial robots, PLCs, and sensor interfacing. Course structure  Module (Robotics lab) Introduction to Robots and Robotics Jogging and Motion Types Program Creation  Module (Mechatronics lab) Introduction to Mechatronics Introduction to Factory Automation and Sensors Pneumatics in Mechatronics  Module (IOT lab) Familiarization with Development board Arduino Uno and Node MCU To interface LED, Push Button, Buzzer, Relay, Bluetooth module (HC-05). Course Duration 12 Days(Start date:17November 2025) Program fees 500/-(Inclusive GST) Program USP’s Opportunity to work with industry-grade robots, PLCs, and sensors. Key take away Hands on operating exposure on latest industrial machine and PLCs. Batch Size Minimum 10 Nos.
